Competition Categories

Challenge yourself across multiple disciplines of modern medicine and healthcare.

Clinical Skills

The Clinical Skills category bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. Participants will be assessed on their ability to perform essential medical procedures, conduct thorough patient assessments, and execute emergency triage protocols. Competitors will face simulated patient encounters where diagnostic reasoning, bedside manner, and rapid decision-making are just as critical as medical accuracy.

Hands-on Diagnostic

Medical Innovation

Modern healthcare relies on technological and methodological advancements. In this category, students are challenged to design, prototype, and present groundbreaking solutions to contemporary healthcare challenges. Whether developing novel biomedical devices, designing healthcare management software, or conceptualizing new treatment delivery methods, competitors must present comprehensive research, engineering principles, and market viability to a panel of judges.

Research Engineering

Public Health

Medicine extends far beyond individual patient care. The Public Health event requires students to analyze widespread health trends, track epidemiological data, and devise comprehensive community-level interventions. Teams will tackle scenarios involving infectious disease outbreaks, chronic health epidemics, or healthcare resource allocation, presenting structured, evidence-based campaigns designed to improve global or regional health outcomes.

Epidemiology Strategy

Healthcare Debate

The medical field is fraught with complex ethical, legal, and economic dilemmas. This event requires profound critical thinking and articulate public speaking. Participants will engage in rigorous, structured debates on pressing topics such as bioethics, healthcare policy reform, patent laws on life-saving medications, and resource rationing in crisis environments. Success relies on deep research, logical structuring, and persuasive delivery.

Policy Bioethics

Anatomy & Physiology

A profound understanding of the human body is the cornerstone of all medical practice. This highly rigorous academic event tests competitors on advanced theoretical knowledge of cellular biology, organ systems, neuroanatomy, and pathology. Through comprehensive written assessments and anatomical identification stations, students must demonstrate mastery of how complex biological systems interact to maintain homeostasis and respond to disease.

Academic Biology
